Mini Rugby Sessions for 5 - 10 Year Olds

Build confidence in your young boys and girls, whilst developing agility, co-ordination and fitness through training sessions.


Instill the values of Teamwork, Respect, Enjoyment, Discipline and Sportsmanship, improving their life skills from playing together.
